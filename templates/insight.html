Eco-Friendly Living Tips

  1. Switch to reusable alternatives instead of single-use plastics.
  2. Buy energy-efficient appliances and use LED lighting.
  3. Dispose of electronics responsibly at certified collection points.
  4. Compost organic waste to reduce landfill pressure.
  5. Use bicycles or public transport to reduce your carbon footprint.
  6. Repair or donate electronics instead of throwing them away.
